Dedication

To Jesus Christ,

the only true source of all authority. Every page of this book is offered in gratitude to the One who never abused His authority, never exalted Himself above others, and never used His power to condemn those who came seeking mercy. Through His life, death, and resurrection, He revealed that true authority is always joined with humility, compassion, and sacrificial love.

This book is also dedicated to every believer who has been wounded by the misuse of spiritual authority. To those who have been judged before they were heard, silenced before they were understood, or burdened by expectations Christ never placed upon them, may these pages remind you that your identity is found in Him and not in the opinions of people.

May every spiritual gift be surrendered to the Lord who gave it. May every position, title, ministry, and platform always point back to Jesus Christ. May every reader be encouraged to pursue humility before influence, faithfulness before recognition, and obedience before applause.

May all authority return to the One to whom it has always belonged.

With gratitude,

Bradley Hobbs
