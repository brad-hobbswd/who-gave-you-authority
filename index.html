Who Gave You Authority?

Authority is one of the most discussed subjects within the Church, yet it is often one of the least understood. Throughout Scripture, authority is never portrayed as something to be pursued for personal recognition or seized through ambition. It is entrusted by God to those who first learn humility, faithfulness, and service. This book examines what the Bible teaches about spiritual authority, servant leadership, discernment, and the lordship of Jesus Christ, inviting every reader to return to the only authority that never fails, the authority of God's Word.
