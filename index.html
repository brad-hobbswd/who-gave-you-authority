Who Gives Authority?

Read the complete manuscript online and discover a thoughtful biblical examination of spiritual authority, servant leadership, humility, discernment, and the lordship of Jesus Christ. This book explores the difference between influence and divine appointment while continually pointing readers back to the One from whom all authority flows.

Whether you are serving in ministry, leading others, or simply seeking a deeper understanding of Scripture, this journey invites you to examine every claim of authority through the truth of God's Word and the example of Jesus Christ.
