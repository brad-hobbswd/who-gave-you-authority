Who Gives Authority?

Read the complete manuscript online and journey through a thoughtful biblical examination of spiritual authority, servant leadership, humility, discernment, and the lordship of Jesus Christ. This book challenges modern assumptions about leadership by returning to the authority of Scripture and revealing that true authority is never seized but always entrusted by God.

Whether you are serving in ministry, leading your family, teaching others, or simply seeking a deeper understanding of God's Word, this book invites you to examine every claim of authority in the light of Jesus Christ, the One to whom all authority in heaven and earth belongs.
